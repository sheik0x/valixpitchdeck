## Presentation Tips

### **Timing:**
- **Full Deck:** 15-20 minutes
- **Quick Pitch:** 5 minutes (Slides 1-5, 8, 14, 17)
- **Deep Dive:** 30 minutes (All slides + Q&A)

### **Delivery:**
- Start with problem (Slide 2) - create urgency
- Emphasize three features (Slide 5) - differentiation
- Show traction (Slide 11) - credibility
- End with vision (Slide 15) - inspiration

### **Q&A Preparation:**
- Technical questions: Reference architecture (Slide 6)
- Market questions: Reference opportunity (Slide 3)
- Competition: Reference landscape (Slide 10)
- Traction: Reference milestones (Slide 11)
